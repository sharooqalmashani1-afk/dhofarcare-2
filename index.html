About DhofarCare

DhofarCare is designed to support digital transformation in Dhofar by giving local service providers (cleaning, maintenance, beauty, logistics, tourism add-ons) a single platform to reach customers. The platform empowers women, youth, and small SMEs with visibility, online booking, and e-payments.

Real-World Problem

Customers in Dhofar struggle to find reliable and verified home services. Providers don't have a digital marketplace.

Opportunity

A cloud and AI-based platform to connect both sides, increase trust, and create income for local entrepreneurs.

Target Users

Families, working women, university housing, offices, villas, and tourists during Khareef.
